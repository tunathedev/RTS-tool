SA50 RTS Ready

The bakery RTS team's tool for dating, pulling, and setting the floor — on your phone, synced to everyone.

It replaces the paper shelf-life sheet: it calculates every sell-by date, builds your morning pull list, walks you through pulling at the freezer, and keeps a shared photo record of the floor. Everything syncs live across all devices.

The daily flow

  1. Open the app and enter the team PIN (ask your lead).
  2. Set the Dating date at the top — every sell-by is calculated from it.
  3. In Browse, tap ＋ on what you need to pull. Press & hold ＋ to flag a 🕳️ hole (an empty spot to fill first).
  4. Open Pull List — it jumps into 🧊 Freezer Mode: a big, glove-friendly checklist grouped by table, holes first. Tap the circle as you pull each item.
  5. Date the product with the sell-by shown — standard shelf life on the floor, or the ❄️ freezer life if it stays frozen.
  6. Check Production for platters & crème-cake builds that your pull list triggers.
  7. At end of day, open the 📸 Floor Log and snap an Arrive and Leave photo — your dated proof the floor was set.

What's inside

📖 Flip BookFlip through shelf-life lengths to see today's resulting date (MM/DD) for each group.
🧊 Freezer ModeFull-screen, big-text pull checklist by table so nothing gets missed at the freezer.
🕳️ HolesFlag empty floor spots; they rise to a "fill first" list and auto-add to your pull list.
📸 Floor LogBefore/after floor photos with a burned-in date stamp, shareable as one proof sheet.
❄️ Freezer lifeEach item can carry a freezer shelf-life separate from its thawed/floor date.
🔖 PLU + scanLabel codes on every item, plus a built-in barcode scanner to look products up.
☁️ Global syncEdits, pull list, and logs update live on every phone — one shared source of truth.
⛅ Weather & tipsLocal San Antonio forecast with sell-through tips for the day.

Get it on your phone

Scan the code → tap the link → tap Share then Add to Home Screen. It installs like a real app with the SA50 icon and works offline. First-time users get an Add to Home Screen prompt automatically.
